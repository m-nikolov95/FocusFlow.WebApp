## Design idea for FocusFlow

When designing, the first questions I ask myself are:

1.What is the general Idea of the application?

2. What is the most important thing to show the user at first glance?

3. How would I need to lay out the components and colors in order to catch attention?

4.What functionalities would the user use most?

After answering these important first questions, I had a general idea of what I would like to show and what would look appealing.
Before I even touched the mouse, the first thing I did was a rough sketch on paper to get a general idea of what I wanted to do.

## Technology Choice

Figma,
React,
TypeScript,
TailwindCSS,

## Assumptions

1. The components and functionalities that the user would mostly like to see in the applications are first.

2. Color schemes and layouts that would not only catch the eye but would have a calming and pleasing effect on the user.

3. While deciding and researching how I would like the UI to look, I decided TailwindCSS would be the best choice to achieve the closest match to my idea, and also the tool to achieve it in the fastest way possible.
